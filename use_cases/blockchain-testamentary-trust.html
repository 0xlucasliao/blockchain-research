Blockchain testamentary trust

Written 10 April 2026 · Last updated 21 April 2026

Problem frame

Testamentary trusts depend on clear intent, reliable custody of keys or signing authority, and eventual alignment with probate or equivalent processes. A chain-based design must say explicitly what is evidenced on-ledger versus what still requires court or trustee discretion.

Questions to resolve in a full note

  • Identity of settlor / testator and revocability windows.
  • Key recovery, social recovery, or institutional custody — and legal standing of each.
  • Oracle or off-chain evidence for death or incapacity triggers.
  • Conflict of laws when domicile and validators differ.
  • Minimization of sensitive personal data on a public ledger.
